Ransomware Tabletop Exercise

Organization

Ubuntu Retail Group (Kenya)

Scenario

BlackCat-style ransomware attack against retail operations.

Participants

- CEO
- COO
- CFO
- General Counsel
- Head of Communications

Escalation Sequence

Injection 1

Point-of-sale outages.

Injection 2

Widespread encryption detected.

Injection 3

Customer data leak threat.

Injection 4

Media pressure and ransom demand.

Outcome

Executive team practiced strategic decision-making during a ransomware crisis.
