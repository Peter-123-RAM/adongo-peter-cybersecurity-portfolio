# Close the Pilot – Founder / CEO

## Role
Founder / Chief Executive Officer (CEO)

## Result
**EXEMPLARY (100/100)**

## Scenario

Led a commercial engagement to secure the first paid cybersecurity pilot with Mwangaza SACCO while competing against a better-funded competitor. The buying committee consisted of the Head of Risk (Champion), Security Lead, CFO, and Procurement. The objective was to understand customer needs, build trust across stakeholders, scope a measurable pilot, price it appropriately, and successfully close the deal.

## Objectives

- Understand the customer's business and security challenges.
- Build trust with every member of the buying committee.
- Design a realistic and measurable pilot engagement.
- Demonstrate business value and return on investment.
- Successfully secure executive approval for the pilot.

## Actions Taken

- Conducted discovery before presenting any solution.
- Identified the SACCO's operational and cybersecurity priorities.
- Won the internal champion by aligning the solution with business risk.
- Demonstrated technical credibility to the Security Lead.
- Presented a clear return on investment to the CFO.
- Worked with Procurement to ensure the commercial process was straightforward.
- Scoped a measurable pilot with defined objectives and success criteria.
- Selected a pricing model appropriate for a first engagement.
- Built momentum across the buying committee.
- Clearly asked for the business and closed the pilot.

## Outcome

- EXEMPLARY (100/100)
- Full buying committee support achieved.
- Discovery completed before solution presentation.
- Measurable pilot approved.
- Commercial pricing accepted.
- Strong stakeholder confidence established.
- Successfully closed the first paid pilot.
