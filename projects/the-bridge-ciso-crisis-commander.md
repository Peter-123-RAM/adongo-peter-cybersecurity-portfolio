The Bridge – CISO Crisis Commander

Role

Chief Information Security Officer (CISO)

Result

EXEMPLARY (100/100)

Scenario

A ransomware incident was actively unfolding within a financial institution. While the Incident Response team managed the technical containment effort, my responsibility as CISO was to lead the crisis bridge, coordinate executive decisions, maintain stakeholder confidence, manage communications, and ensure the organization remained aligned during a rapidly developing incident.

Key Responsibilities

- Executive leadership during crisis response
- Board communication
- Media engagement
- Customer communication
- Internal team leadership
- Disclosure decision-making
- Ransom policy decisions
- Strategic incident management

Actions Performed

Board Management

Provided clear, factual, and timely updates to the Board regarding:

- Incident status
- Business impact
- Response progress
- Risk exposure
- Recovery planning

Team Leadership

Maintained confidence and cohesion within the Incident Response team while ensuring leadership support remained visible throughout the event.

Empowered the IR Lead

Delegated operational authority to the Incident Response Lead rather than interfering with technical response activities.

Member Communication

Addressed customer concerns directly and transparently to reduce uncertainty and maintain confidence.

Media Management

Engaged with the press proactively to prevent misinformation and maintain public trust.

Disclosure Decision

Made an early disclosure decision under organizational control rather than waiting for external pressure.

Ransom Decision

Established a clear organizational policy not to pay the ransom and communicated the recovery strategy.

Outcome

- Board confidence maintained
- Member trust preserved
- Media narrative managed effectively
- Crisis remained controlled and coordinated
- Response teams remained focused and empowered
- Disclosure conducted proactively
- Clear executive decision-making demonstrated

Skills Demonstrated

- Crisis Leadership
- CISO Decision-Making
- Executive Communication
- Stakeholder Management
- Media Relations
- Governance & Risk Management
- Incident Command
- Strategic Leadership
- Organizational Resilience
- Security Program Leadership
