# Raise the Seed – Founder / CEO

## Role
Founder / Chief Executive Officer (CEO)

## Result
**EXEMPLARY (90/100)**

## Scenario

Led a seed fundraising process for a cybersecurity startup with six weeks of runway remaining. Successfully secured commitments from a lead fund, operator angel, strategic investor, and micro-VC while competing against a recently funded rival startup.

## Objectives

- Build investor conviction through measurable traction.
- Tailor the investment case to each investor's thesis.
- Establish clean investment terms.
- Create momentum throughout the round.
- Successfully close investor commitments.

## Actions Taken

- Opened with measurable business traction, including revenue, customer retention, and pipeline growth.
- Won commitment from the Lead Fund by demonstrating market opportunity and execution.
- Built conviction with the Operator Angel through founder execution and operational discipline.
- Aligned the Strategic Investor around long-term market fit and partnership value.
- Secured commitment from the Micro-VC after establishing lead investor confidence.
- Set clear investment terms and an agreed closing timeline once investor conviction was established.
- Called for the close to convert commitments into an executable funding round.

## Outcome

- EXEMPLARY (90/100)
- All four investors committed.
- Round built on measurable traction rather than narrative alone.
- Investment terms established with a defined closing date.
- Strong investor momentum maintained throughout the fundraising process.
