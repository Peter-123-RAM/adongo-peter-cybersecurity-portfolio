The Leaver – Insider Threat Incident Response

Role

Incident Commander – Insider Threat

Result

EXEMPLARY (100/100)

Scenario

A senior software engineer resigned from Sahili Pay and attempted to exfiltrate sensitive customer information before leaving the organization. Data Loss Prevention (DLP) alerts detected bulk copying of customer exports while the user's access remained active.

As Incident Commander, my responsibility was to rapidly contain the insider threat, preserve evidence, support legal action, protect customer information, and keep executive leadership informed.

Objectives

- Stop data exfiltration.
- Secure critical business systems.
- Preserve forensic evidence.
- Support legal and HR investigations.
- Meet regulatory notification requirements.
- Maintain executive awareness.

Actions Performed

Identity Containment

- Disabled the departing engineer's account.
- Disabled the malicious mail auto-forwarding rule.

System Protection

- Locked access to:
  - Customer Database
  - S3 Exports
  - Production Repository

Evidence Preservation

Captured and preserved:

- DLP alerts
- Endpoint artefacts
- Mailbox evidence
- User activity logs

Legal Readiness

Issued a legal hold to preserve evidence for HR and potential law enforcement proceedings.

Executive Communication

Provided timely and accurate updates to executive leadership throughout the incident.

Regulatory Compliance

Ensured notification obligations were fulfilled within the required legal timeframe.

Outcome

- EXAMPLARY score: 100/100
- Data exfiltration prevented before meaningful customer information was lost.
- All critical systems secured.
- Forensic evidence preserved.
- Legal case protected.
- Executive leadership remained informed.
- Regulatory requirements satisfied.
