# Win the Mandate – CISO Board Strategy

## Role
Chief Information Security Officer (CISO)

## Result
**EXEMPLARY (90/100)**

## Scenario

Led an executive board strategy session to secure funding, authority, and executive sponsorship for a cybersecurity program. The board included the CEO, CFO, COO, and Audit Chair, each with different business priorities and concerns.

## Objectives

- Build executive confidence.
- Justify cybersecurity investment using quantified business risk.
- Present a realistic implementation roadmap.
- Secure approval for the cybersecurity program.

## Actions Taken

- Addressed the CEO with a business growth and resilience strategy.
- Demonstrated cybersecurity ROI and financial risk reduction to the CFO.
- Explained operational resilience and business continuity benefits to the COO.
- Provided governance, compliance, and assurance evidence to the Audit Chair.
- Grounded recommendations in measurable risk rather than fear.
- Presented a phased, costed cybersecurity roadmap.
- Requested the board's approval with a clear executive decision.

## Outcome

- EXEMPLARY (90/100)
- Achieved unanimous executive support.
- Increased board confidence in the security strategy.
- Secured momentum for the cybersecurity programme.
- Successfully communicated security as a business enabler rather than a technical cost.
